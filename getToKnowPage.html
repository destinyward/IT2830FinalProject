Meet our Executive Board

Click on each member's profile to view their position

Nora Sieckhaus

President

Nora Sieckhaus

St. Louis, MO

The President is a liaison for the Chapter concerning all community and campus activities. The President will represent the Chapter at both National Committee conventions and will speak to the WGA about any matter they deem necessary.

Megan Martin

VP of New Scholars

Megan Martin

St. Louis, MO

During the New Scholar semester, the New Scholar Educator is to act as a liaison between the Active Members and the New Scholar Class, and to carry out the desires of the Chapter concerning New Scholar Activities.

Mackenzie Esposito

Executive Vice President

Mackenzie Esposito

Bowie, MD

The EVP is in full charge of running the Annual Evans Scholars Philanthropies and will also bring proposals for all other philanthropies to the Chapter.

Collin Rooney

VP of Administration

Collin Rooney

Wentzville, IL

The AVP will supervise all repairs and house improvement projects. The AVP will be responsible for purchasing house supplies, inspecting for fire and safety hazards, and assign house jobs.

David Stern

VP of Finance

David Stern

Glenview, IL

The VP of Finance is in charge of establishing a rate of dues that will be sufficient to cover the costs of the Chapter’s utilities and minor maintenance. The VP of Finance also budgets the annual expenses.

Destiny Ward

VP of Communications

Destiny Ward

Dolton, IL

The VP of Communication oversees the chapter’s website and social media efforts. This position coordinates all house correspondence and is responsible for creating a professional and welcoming image of their Evans Scholars chapter.


Brandi McManigal

VP of Diversity and Inclusion

Brandi McManigal

Oak Forest, IL

The VP of Diversity and Inclusion strives for equity of all backgrounds. The purpose of this position is to increase our awareness and understanding of ourselves and the communities we serve.
